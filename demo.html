M.N. Dyeing, Printing & Washing Mills Ltd. M.N. Dyeing, Printing and Washing Mills Limited is a project of Mosharaf Group located at Bhabanipur in Gazipur with a total floor area of 374,000 sq ft. Mosharaf Group is one of the leading international trade, commerce, and industry organizations in Bangladesh, established in 1993.It has a per day capacity of Twenty Five (25) Tons of Knitting. Thirty Five (35) Tons of Knit dyeing. Ten (10) Tons of Yarn dyeing, Four (4) Tons of Fiber dyeing facilities, Fifteen (15) Tons of Fabric printing Facilities and Fifty Thousand (50,000) Pieces of Garments Printing facilities. M.N. Dyeing, Printing & Washing Mills Ltd. has a vertical setup for complete process of fabric. Along with our Yarn manufacturing unit, we are capable of providing complete support from yarn to ready dyed and printed fabric for 100% Export oriented Garment industries.
